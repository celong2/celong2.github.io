  • About Me
After high school, I served two years in the Navy and then spent a summer doing some hiking on the Appalachian trail before attending St. Mary's College of Maryland. When I graduated, I traveled around Europe and Morocco and then taught English in Korea for a year. Eventually, I went to graduate school and received my Ph.D. in Mathematics from NC State University in May of 2016. Currently, I am a Postdoctoral Research Fellow at the Mathematical Biosciences Institute in Columbus, Ohio. In my spare time I enjoy playing soccer, running, being outdoors, and hanging out with my wife and cats.
